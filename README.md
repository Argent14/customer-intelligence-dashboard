# 📊 Customer Intelligence & Revenue Growth Dashboard

## 🚀 Overview
This project presents an executive-level Power BI dashboard designed to analyze customer behavior, revenue performance, and churn risk.

It transforms raw transactional data into actionable business insights.

---

## 🎯 Key Features

- Customer Segmentation (VIP, Growth, At Risk, Low Value)
- Revenue Distribution Analysis
- Churn Risk Identification
- Revenue Drivers (Region, Category, Discounts)
- Strategic Business Recommendations

## 💡 Key Insights

- A small group of customers drives a significant portion of revenue
- High-value customers are at risk of churn
- Discounts do not always lead to higher revenue
- Revenue performance varies significantly across regions and categories
